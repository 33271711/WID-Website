Who We are
Women in Dentistry Society (WA) is a non-profit organisation focused on supporting dentistry and community. We provide opportunities for West Australian dentists, dental specialists and dental students to meet likeminded individuals and share the experiences and challenges unique to women in dentistry, whilst celebrating the strength of women and encouraging positive change within the profession and community.
